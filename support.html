Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't I see any available slots?
Availability is fetched directly from the providers. If no slots are shown, all courts are currently booked. Use slot alerts to get notified when one becomes available.
How do slot alerts work?
Tap the bell icon, create an alert with a date, time range, and optionally a location. You'll receive a push notification when a matching slot becomes available.
I'm not receiving push notifications
Make sure notifications for Padello are enabled in your system settings (Settings â†’ Padello â†’ Notifications).
Can I book directly in the app?
Padello shows you availability. To book, you'll be redirected to the provider's website (Padeldome, Playtomic, Eversports, etc.).
Which regions are supported?
Currently: Vienna, Lower Austria, Upper Austria, Salzburg, Styria, Carinthia, Tyrol, Vorarlberg, Burgenland, and Munich.
